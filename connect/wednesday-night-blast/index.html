Wednesday Night "B.L.A.S.T."

A weekly midweek gathering for kids and families that keeps the church's Wednesday rhythm clear, joyful, and easy to follow.

View calendar
Wednesday nights6:30-7:30 pmSeptember-November and January-April.

Overview

A family night with a clear midweek anchor.

B.L.A.S.T. is built to give children a place to learn, sing, play, and grow while parents have a simple Wednesday rhythm to follow.

The B.L.A.S.T. begins with classes for all ages from 6:30-7:30 pm. The goal is not to fill a night with noise, but to create a weekly gathering that helps kids experience God in age-appropriate ways.

Children move through music and worship time, interactive lessons, games, crafts, and small-group time. It is a simple structure, but it gives Wednesday nights a consistent shape that families can trust.

What Wednesday feels like

Music

Worship time opens the night.

Children experience God through singing and a shared time of praise before the lesson and activities begin.

Lesson

Interactive teaching keeps the night engaging.

The ministry uses age-appropriate lessons so children can hear truth in a way that is simple and memorable.

Play

Games and crafts are built into the evening.

B.L.A.S.T. gives kids room to move, make, and enjoy the night while staying inside a gospel-shaped rhythm.

Groups

Small-group time closes the night.

The last part of the evening helps kids connect with leaders and process what they learned together.

Schedule

The Wednesday rhythm is easy to follow.

These are the essentials families need when they arrive on Wednesday night.

Season

September through November, then January through April.

B.L.A.S.T. follows the school-year rhythm so Wednesday nights stay connected to the church calendar and the family schedule.

Time

Classes begin at 6:30 pm and end at 7:30 pm.

All ages gather during the same window so families can arrive once, settle in, and move through the night together.

Location

Children finish in the Emmanuel Kids Wing.

Pickup is handled where kids already know the space and leaders can send them home in an orderly way.

Sunday tie-in

Children's Church and family care are part of the same rhythm.

The ministry is designed to support children during both Sunday services and to give parents a private place to care for little ones.

Age groups

Each room is set up for a specific age range.

Families can drop children in the correct room quickly and know where pickup will happen at the end of the night.

Nursery

Room 102

6 weeks to 2 years old.

Pre-K

Rooms 101 and 103

Ages 3-5 years.

Elementary

Worship Center, then kids hall

Kids begin together, then split into age groups with small-group time at the end.

Family care

The whole evening is designed with families in mind.

Children's Church, a nursing moms room, and a clear contact person keep the night accessible for parents and caregivers.

Children's Church

Both Sunday services

Children participate in praise and worship and hear a lesson tailored for them during both services every week.

Nursing moms

Room 210

A private room where parents can watch the live stream of each service with a little one.

Contact

Rachel Bishop

Children's Pastor and Preschool Director who helps families find the right room and the right next step.
