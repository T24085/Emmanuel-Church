Overview

A children's ministry built around worship, learning, and belonging.

Emmanuel Kids helps children know Jesus, grow in community, and feel seen by the church family around them.

Emmanuel Kids is intentionally woven into the Sunday schedule and the broader family rhythms of Emmanuel Church.

The ministry covers nursery, pre-K, and elementary age groups, and the programming is organized so families know exactly where to go.

From Sunday morning worship to midweek B.L.A.S.T., the goal is the same: help children grow in a way that is joyful, safe, and gospel-focused.

Love

Kids experience the love of Christ in a warm, welcoming setting.

Children are known, helped, and encouraged by leaders who care about their growth and joy.

Grace

Grace shows up in how children are welcomed and taught.

The ministry is designed to help kids learn in age-appropriate ways while feeling safe and seen.

Worship

Worship is part of the rhythm, not a side activity.

Singing, Bible teaching, and shared prayer all shape the kids ministry experience.

Truth

Children hear clear biblical truth in a form they can understand.

The ministry aims to help kids experience God with teaching that is simple, faithful, and memorable.

Wednesday Night

B.L.A.S.T. gives kids a strong midweek anchor.

The ministry runs on a seasonal schedule and brings children together for teaching, music, games, and small groups.

Wednesday Night B.L.A.S.T. runs September through November and January through April.

Classes meet from 6:30-7:30 pm for nursery, pre-K, and elementary kids.

Children experience music and worship, interactive lessons, games, crafts, and small-group time.

All children may be picked up in the Emmanuel Kids Wing.
